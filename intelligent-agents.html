Final Reflection

My learning journey through Intelligent Agent Systems

WHAT?

Throughout the Intelligent Agent Systems module, I engaged with a range of concepts that deepened my understanding of agent-based computing and its real-world applications. My final project, "A Modular Multi-Agent AI System for Anomaly Detection in Fitbit Data," synthesised this learning.

Read full reflection

SO WHAT?

One pivotal moment came during the evaluation of our MAS architecture. Initial designs proposed a centralized model, but team feedback raised concerns about scalability. We pivoted to a multi-agent architecture, teaching me the value of adaptive planning and iterative co-design.

Read full reflection

NOW WHAT?

Looking ahead, this module has laid a strong foundation for my future in AI. I now feel confident in developing modular AI architectures and understand the value of structuring agents around autonomy and communication. I plan to pursue further reading on explainable AI (XAI).
