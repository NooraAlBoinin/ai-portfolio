Final Reflection

The lesson on machine learning has completely changed the way I think about things, from basic ideas to real-life examples. I didn't know much about machine learning at first, but now I'm comfortable with the different methods and how they affect my professional, social, and ethical life.

WHAT

Hands-on activities helped me learn how AI and ML are changing businesses. Working on a CNN for the CIFAR-10 dataset was challenging yet rewarding. The Boeing 737 Max case study showed the dangers of flawed automated systems.

SO WHAT

I realized that fairness, transparency, and accountability are as important as model accuracy. Biased datasets in our Unit 6 project showed how ML can reinforce social biases if not used properly.

NOW WHAT

I plan to apply these learnings to more complex ML projects, focusing on unsupervised and reinforcement learning. I'll prioritize ethical considerations in all future projects, ensuring models are transparent and accountable.

Key Takeaways

  • Mastered complex algorithms like CNNs and understood their ethical implications
  • Developed strong teamwork skills in virtual environments
  • Gained practical experience with real-world datasets and problems
  • Learned the importance of model evaluation and selection

Future Goals

  • Explore unsupervised learning and reinforcement learning techniques
  • Apply ML in finance and healthcare sectors
  • Continue developing ethical AI practices
  • Contribute to open-source ML projects
